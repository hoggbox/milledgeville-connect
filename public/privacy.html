Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, email, and profile picture when you sign up
  • Posts, comments, marketplace listings, lost & found items
  • Location data (optional) for better local experience
  • Push notification token (for alerts)
  • Usage data to improve the app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide community features (traffic alerts, marketplace, etc.)
  • Send relevant push notifications
  • Moderate content and prevent abuse
  • Improve the app based on usage

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information is only shared when necessary for app functionality (e.g., your marketplace listing is visible to other users in Milledgeville).

4. Data Retention

Traffic alerts auto-delete after 8 hours. You can delete your account and data at any time from the profile page.

5. Your Rights

You can request to see, correct, or delete your data by contacting us at ghogg86@gmail.com.
